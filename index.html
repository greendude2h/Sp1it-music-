Commands

Name Description Usage
!play It allows you to open music on Youtube. !play "sound-name or link"
!stop Stops the music. !stop
!lyrics Get lyrics for the currently playing song. !lyrics
!loop Toggle music loop. !loop
!resume Resume currently playing music. !resume
!queue Show music queue. !queue
!nowplaying Show now playing song. !nowplaying or !np
!remove Remove song from the queue. !remove
!search Search and select videos to play. !search "video name"
!skip Skip the currently playing song. !skip
!skipto Skip to the selected queue number. !skipto "queue number"
!pause Pause playing sound. !pause
!volume Change volume of currently playing music. !volume "0 - 150"
!playlist Play a playlist from youtube. !playlist "name or link"
!shuffle Shuffle the queue. !shuffle
